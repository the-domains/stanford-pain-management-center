About the Pain Management Center We are a team of providers from multiple specialties, from Anesthesiology, Internal Medicine, Neurology, Psychiatry, Physical Medicine, Psychology, and Physical Therapy. We work with patients with chronic pain using gentle medications, targeted interventions, individualized psychological therapy, and guided physical therapy.

About Dr. Ming-Chih Kao, PhD, MD Dr. Kao studied psychology, biology, mathematics, and computational biology at Harvard. He consulted for the NIH and received MD at Michigan. With additional clinical experience as a primary care physician, Dr. Kao’s approach to chronic pain is scientific, patient-centric, and most importantly, coordinated with the patient’s whole care team.
